Alerts when something's not right

Firstly, meaningful alerts with follow-on actions let you know about an issue before there's loss of data. Selected support technicians get this alert and can start the process.

SiteLink Dashboard gives the overview

SiteLink gets a snapshot of all IoT devices across a site to give quick situational awareness and pinpoint how widespread an issue is.

DeviceLink gives information for devices which have lost internet access

DeviceLink provides any onsite electrician with the ability to find out information about devices which have lost internet connectivity. You'll quickly know which area has failed.

Instant access to support when you can't find the fault

A unique "Request call now" button allows you to get immediate backup from an on-call technician while you're there with a device. Expect a phone call within 15 minutes of requesting help.
